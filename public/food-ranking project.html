Jana's Favorite food

Accprding to Jana the best two are:

Fettuccine

Fettuccine Alfredo is an Italian pasta dish made with wide, flat noodles mixed in a creamy sauce of butter, cream, and Parmesan cheese. It’s known for its rich, smooth texture and comforting flavor, often served with chicken or shrimp.

Kabsa

Kabsa is a traditional Saudi rice dish cooked with a blend of spices, meat (such as chicken, lamb, or fish), tomatoes, and vegetables. It’s famous for its aromatic flavors and is usually served as a main dish at family gatherings and special occasions.
